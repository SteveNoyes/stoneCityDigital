Lead Graphics

I have a Bachelor of Science Degree in Graphic Design, minor in Photography and Illustrations. Over 8 years of experience in the design industry designing for PRINT / WEB. Proficient in Adobe CS6 - Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign.

I have been drawing / painting since I was a child. I can have a blank canvas and make it come to life in whatever medium: graphite, charcoal, ink, color pastels, oil pastels, oil paint, acrylic paint, water colors, and digital painting.

While attending The Art Institute of Las Vegas, my minor was in Photography. Having professional experience working as a full time photographer at the Grand Canyon West (Skywalk). Shot for various clients for weddings, concerts at the House of Blues, boudoir photoshoots, marketing materials, family events, etc.
